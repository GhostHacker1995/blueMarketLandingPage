What BlueMarket is!

BlueMarket is a service that seeks to bridge the gap between clients with blue-collar job opportunities and skilled workers. We seek to provide the services you need quicker and with more finesse. This app seeks to streamline the process of finding, hiring and managing blue-collar services while transforming the way people engage with these skilled traders by engaging service providers and skilled workers.

Whether you need a plumber, electrician, carpenter, or any other skilled worker or raw material, BlueMarket connects users with experienced professionals, making it easy to get the job done efficiently and effectively

Features

Why we stand out

User-Friendly Interface

BlueMarket boasts an intuitive and user-friendly interface that caters to both job seekers and service providers.
The app's clean and modern design ensures a seamless experience for users of all ages and technological backgrounds.

Job Postings and Search

Job seekers can browse through a wide array of job listings posted by service providers. They can filter jobs based on location, skill type, and availability, enabling them to find opportunities that match their expertise and schedule

Detailed Profiles

Service providers create detailed profiles showcasing their skills, qualifications, work history, and customer reviews. This comprehensive information empowers users to make informed decisions when selecting a service provider.

Real-Time Communication

The app features a real-time messaging system that allows users and service providers to communicate directly. This functionality ensures clear and instant communication, helping users discuss job requirements, negotiate terms, and stay updated on project progres



Secure Payment Gateway

BlueMarket integrates a secure payment gateway that facilitates hassle-free transactions between users and service providers. Users can make payments within the app, promoting transparency and accountability in financial matters.

Ratings and Reviews

After completing a job, users can rate and provide reviews for the service provider. This feedback system encourages quality service and helps other users make informed decisions when hiring professionals.

Advertising and educational videos.

The app includes a feature that allows for posting 15 second adverts of your products, DIY videos as well and any beneficial videos that you wish to share to the BlueMarket communitry

Job Scheduling and Reminders

Service providers can manage their schedules by indicating their availability on the app's calendar feature. Users can also set reminders for upcoming jobs to ensure they never miss an important appointment.



Geo-Location Services

The app utilises geo-location services to connect users with service providers in their vicinity. This ensures that users can quickly find professionals who are geographically convenient.

Skill Verification

BlueMarket offers an option for service providers to undergo skill verification, where their qualifications and certifications are confirmed. This gives users an added layer of confidence when selecting a professional.

Customer Support:

he app includes a responsive customer support feature that assists users with any queries, concerns, or technical issues they may encounter while using the platform.
